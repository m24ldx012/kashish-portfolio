Hailing from Mumbai, I didn't just grow up in a city; I grew up in a masterclass of complex systems. It forged my belief that the most complicated systems can—and must—be designed to be intuitive. Today, I use the interdisciplinary lens of an architect, an entrepreneur, and a UX designer to capture the bird’s-eye view of complex digital systems.
