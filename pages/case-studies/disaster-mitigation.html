Overview: Accelerating Response with Data

In disaster scenarios, every second counts. This project focused on creating a centralized dashboard for government officials to visualize real-time needs and allocate funds based on data-driven priorities rather than ad-hoc requests.

Why is fund allocation problematic?

Current systems rely on manual reporting chains which delay funding by days. Our platform automates the request flow, providing instant validation and approval pathways for emergency procurement.

Process Flow Diagram (Placeholder)

The Solution

We designed a map-based interface that overlays request density with infrastructure vulnerability data. This allows decision-makers to identify hotspots instantly and direct resources where they prevent cascading failures.
